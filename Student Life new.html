On Campus Accommodation

Dormitories provide on-campus housing with fully furnished rooms, available as either singles or doubles. Unlike many off-campus apartments and a few other dormitory options, the accommodation fee covers all utilities, including electricity, water, and internet, making it a convenient and cost-effective choice for students.

Pros Cons
Convenient access to campus facilities Less privacy in shared dorms
All utilities included in the fee Potential for noise and disturbances
No additional commuting costs Limited freedom in guest policies

Off Campus Accommodation

Students may choose to live off campus in apartments, which often provide more independence and flexibility. These apartments can vary in size and amenities, allowing students to select accommodations that suit their lifestyle and budget.

Pros Cons
More privacy and independence Utilities are not included
Variety of housing options Farther from campus facilities

Transportation Services

Universities in Northern Cyprus provide shuttle buses specifically for their students, transporting them from their residences to various campus locations, including classrooms and other facilities. These buses operate on a scheduled timetable, ensuring students can plan their commutes conveniently.

Food Services

Universities here also offer a variety of on-campus dining options, including restaurants and cafeterias. These facilities serve a wide range of dishes, with options that span from traditional Turkish cuisine to international meals, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.

Health Services

Universities also ensure students have access to free basic healthcare services. This includes general medical attention, outpatient treatment, and care for minor illnesses or injuries, providing students with essential support for their health and well-being.

Campus Security

Safety is a top priority for international students, and North Cyprus is known for being a safe destination. The country has a low crime rate, and the local community is welcoming and supportive of international students. Universities in North Cyprus also have security measures in place to ensure the safety of their students. However, it is always important to exercise caution and follow basic safety guidelines, as you would in any other country.

Food in Northern Cyprus

As previously mentioned, universities in Northern Cyprus provide a variety of cafeterias and restaurants, offering students a range of dining options from luxurious to budget-friendly establishments. This flexibility allows students to tailor their dining experiences according to their preferences and financial situations. However, many Ugandan students opt to prepare meals from home as a strategic way to manage their expenses. Common staples include groundnuts, rice, beans, and posho, which can be enhanced with a variety of spices. By utilizing the cooking facilities in their dorm rooms or apartments, these students can enjoy traditional meals while effectively minimizing the costs associated with external dining options.

Cost of living in Northern Cyprus

The cost of living in North Cyprus can vary depending on individual preferences and lifestyle choices. On average, international students can expect to spend between $350 and $500 per month. This amount covers expenses such as accommodation, meals, transportation, and other daily necessities. However, it is important to note that these figures are approximate and can vary depending on personal spending habits.

Why study in Northern Cyprus

Living in North Cyprus as an international student offers a unique and enriching experience. The affordable cost of living, vibrant cultural scene, and diverse social activities make it an attractive destination for students from around the world. With a range of housing options, access to quality healthcare, and numerous entertainment opportunities, students can make the most of their time in North Cyprus. Whether you are interested in pursuing your studies or simply exploring a new culture, North Cyprus has much to offer.
